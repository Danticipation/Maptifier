Maptifier Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Maptifier ("the App") is a projection mapping application for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data We Collect

The App collects the following anonymous data to improve the user experience:

  • Crash reports — via Firebase Crashlytics. Includes device model, OS version, and stack trace. No personal information.
  • Usage analytics — via Firebase Analytics. Anonymous events such as app opens, features used, and session duration. No personal information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No user accounts or login credentials
  • No location data
  • No contacts or call logs
  • No photos, videos, or media content (all media stays on your device)
  • No advertising identifiers

Data Storage

All projects, media, and settings are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to our servers. Crash reports and analytics are processed by Google Firebase under their privacy policy.

Data Retention

Crash reports are retained by Firebase for 90 days. Analytics data is retained for 14 months, then automatically deleted.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Analytics

Your Rights

You can disable analytics data collection in the App's Settings screen. You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App or clearing its data in Android Settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated "Everyone" and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
